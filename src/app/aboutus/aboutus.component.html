smart dustbins

Smart dustbin is one of those innovative ideas. The smart dustbin uses an Ultrasonic sensor HC-SR04 to detect when the

waste bin reaches the maximum level.It then sends the signals to Arduino Uno.This will alert both the Trash man and the

city office.Then the Trash Man collects the waste from every active hotspots following the route given for him.After the waste

is collected, hotspot will be disabled automatically. If in case,if the waste in the area wise waste bin is not increasing or

remains same for 2days then a hotspot will be enabled automatically.If in case, the Trash Man missed collecting the waste from any

active hotspot in that area a message will be sent to the city office.Then the city office will instruct the Trash Man to collect

the dust from that active hotspot.Finally the waste is moved to the dump yard.Then at the dump yard the segregation process will be

done.
